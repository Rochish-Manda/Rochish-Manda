About -

Intro

I’m now a fourth- and final-year undergrad (B.Tech) majoring in Metallurgical & Materials Engineering at IITKGP, who has the passion & will to strive for the vision of empowering everyone lacking access to affordable energy sources - with dirt-cheap sustainable energy. Much of my recent work has been on solid-state Li & Na Ion Batteries. In continuation of my current research, I would love to work on high amperage battery systems which -
1. Have higher energy efficiencies at high C-rates over significantly higher number of discharge cycles.
2. Are made out of dirt-cheap functional materials.

I love travelling and encountering different cultures, people & their perspectives. During my third year I had the opportunity to work at University of Alberta, Canada.
